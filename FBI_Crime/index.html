Hate crimes are crimes that manifest evidence of prejudice based on Race, Gender , Gender Identity, Religion, Disability, Sexual Orientation, and Ethnicity.

According to the agencies of each state which reported one or more Hate Crime incidents occurred in their respective jurisdictions during 2013, there are

5798 Total Deaths

2732 crimes caused by Racial Bias

1271 crimes caused by Sexual Oriented Bias

910 crimes caused by Religious Bias

680 crimes caused by Ethnical Bias

50 crimes caused by Gender Identity Bias

5798 crimes caused by Gender Bias
